The Moulin Rouge

Step into the captivating realm of Moulin Rouge, where glamour and artistry intertwine. This iconic venue has been a symbol of Parisian nightlife since 1889, showcasing a dazzling array of performances that enchant audiences from around the globe.

Browse by topic

History

Founded in 1889, Moulin Rouge emerged as a cultural hub for entertainment and art. It became the birthplace of the can-can dance, attracting artists like Toulouse-Lautrec who immortalized its lively atmosphere through their works.

Iconic Can-Can

The Can-Can dance is synonymous with Moulin Rouge, characterized by its energetic kicks and flamboyant costumes. This iconic performance embodies the spirit of liberty and celebration, captivating audiences with its boldness and choreography.

The Art of Cabaret

Cabaret at Moulin Rouge is an art form that combines storytelling with performance. The shows often feature humor, drama, and seduction, providing a unique glimpse into the Parisian lifestyle and its rich cultural tapestry.

Culinary Delights

Dining at Moulin Rouge is an experience in itself. Guests are treated to a feast of French cuisine, perfectly paired with champagne. The culinary journey enhances the sensory experience of the show, making it truly memorable.
